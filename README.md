Marketing Analytics Dashboard

1.About the Project
This project is a Marketing Analytics Dashboard created using Power BI. The dashboard analyzes customer behavior, marketing campaign performance, customer satisfaction, discounts, and revenue trends to understand what drives customer spending and repeat purchases.
The project focuses on helping businesses make better marketing and operational decisions using data.
2. Objectives
- Analyze customer spending patterns
- Measure campaign effectiveness
- Understand the impact of discounts on sales
- Study customer satisfaction and repeat purchases
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Evaluate how delivery time affects customer experience
3. Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Data Visualization
4.Dashboard Visualizations
4.1.KPI Cards
The dashboard includes important business metrics such as:
- Total Revenue
- Total Customers
- Average Spend
- Repeat Purchase Rate
These KPIs provide a quick summary of overall business performance.
4.2.Revenue by City and Campaign Type
This chart compares revenue generated across different cities and marketing campaigns.
It helps identify:
- Top-performing cities
- Most successful campaigns
- Regional customer trends
4.3.Impact of Discounts on Spending
A scatter plot was used to analyze how discounts affect customer spending behavior.
This helps understand whether higher discounts lead to increased purchases.
4.4.Revenue by Membership Type
This visualization shows how different customer groups contribute to total revenue.
The analysis found that Premium customers generated the highest revenue.
4.5.Campaign Effectiveness
This chart compares campaign performance based on customer purchasing behavior.
It helps identify which campaigns perform best for different customer segments.
4.5.Delivery Time vs Customer Satisfaction
This analysis studies how delivery speed affects customer satisfaction.
The dashboard shows that longer delivery times reduce customer satisfaction.
4.6.Satisfaction vs Repeat Purchases
This visualization shows the relationship between customer satisfaction and repeat buying behavior.
The analysis indicates that satisfied customers are more likely to make repeat purchases.
4.7.Interactive Filters
The dashboard includes filters for:
- City
- Campaign Type
- Membership Type
These filters allow users to explore the data interactively.
4.8.Key Insights
- Customer satisfaction strongly influences repeat purchases
- Premium customers contribute the highest revenue
- Some marketing campaigns perform better than others
- Faster delivery improves customer satisfaction
- Discounts do not always guarantee higher spending
5.Conclusion
This project demonstrates how Power BI can be used to convert raw business data into meaningful insights. The dashboard helps understand
customer behavior, campaign performance, and operational efficiency, allowing businesses to make better data-driven decisions.
